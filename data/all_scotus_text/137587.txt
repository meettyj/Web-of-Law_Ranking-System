    IN RE DISCIPLINE OF VINYARD. No. D-2377.
    Supreme Court of United States.

      It is ordered that Michael C. Vinyard, of Ottumwa, Iowa, be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
