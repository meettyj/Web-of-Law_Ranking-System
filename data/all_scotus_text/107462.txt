      The motion for leave to proceed in forma au peris and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. Ed. 2d 493, and Long v. District Court of Iowa in and for Lee County, 385 U.S. 192, 87 S. Ct. 362, 17 L.Ed.2d 290.
