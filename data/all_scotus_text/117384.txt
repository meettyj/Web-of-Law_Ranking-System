510 U.S. 1174     Louisiana v. Mississippi et al.     No. 121, Orig.     Supreme Court of United States.     March 7, 1994.            11       It is ordered that the Honorable Vincent L. McKusick, Retired Chief Justice, Supreme Judicial Court of Maine, be appointed Special Master in this case with authority to fix the time and conditions for the filing of additional pleadings and to direct subsequent proceedings, and with authority to summon witnesses, issue subpoenas, and take such evidence as may be introduced and such as he may deem it necessary to call for.
