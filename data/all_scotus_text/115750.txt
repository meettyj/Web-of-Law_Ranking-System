510 U.S. 1021
    In re Disbarment of Kristoff. No. D-1335.
    Supreme Court of United States.
    December 13, 1993.
    
      11
      It is ordered that Lawrence E. Kristoff, of White Plains, N. Y., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
