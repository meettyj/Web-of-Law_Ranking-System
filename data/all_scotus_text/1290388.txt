
(2008)
DAVID TABOR 
v. 
UNITED STATES
No. 06-5244
Supreme Court of United States.
January 14, 2008.

The motion of petitioner for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petitions for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the Eighth Circuit for further consideration in light of Kimbrough v. United States, 552 U.S. ___ (2007).
