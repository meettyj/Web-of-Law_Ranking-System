
(2008)
JUSTIN B. HASTE 
v. 
UNITED STATES
No. 07-7412
Supreme Court of United States.
April 21, 2008.

The motion of petitioner for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the Fourth Circuit for further consideration in light of Begay v. United States, 553 U.S. ___ (2008).
