    In re Disbarment of Carver. No. D-1328.
    Supreme Court of United States.

      It is ordered that Thomas Henry Carver, of Beverly Hills, Cal., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
