510 U.S. 1070
    In re Disbarment of Cohen. No. D-1355.
    Supreme Court of United States.
    January 18, 1994.
    
      11
      It is ordered that Allen C. Cohen, of New York, N. Y., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
