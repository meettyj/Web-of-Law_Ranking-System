
(2008)
GUADALUPE A. GARCIA-LARA 
v. 
UNITED STATES
No. 07-9799
Supreme Court of United States.
April 28, 2008.

The motion of petitioner for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the Tenth Circuit for further consideration in light of Gall v. United States, 552 U.S. ___ (2007).
