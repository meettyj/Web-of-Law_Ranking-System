    In re Disbarment of Annin. No. D-1318.

      It is ordered that Roselyn Costa Annin, of Manteca, Cal., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring her to show cause why she should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
