510 U.S. 1105
    In re Disbarment of Sneed. No. D-1362.
    Supreme Court of United States.
    February 22, 1994.
    
      11
      It is ordered that Benjamin Sneed, of New York, N. Y., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
