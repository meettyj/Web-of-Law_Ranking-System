510 U.S. 1174
    In re Disbarment of Cohen. No. D-1355.
    Supreme Court of United States.
    March 7, 1994.
    
      11
      Allen C. Cohen, of New York, N. Y., having requested to resign as a member of the Bar of this Court, it is ordered that his name be stricken from the roll of attorneys admitted to practice before the Bar of this Court. The rule to show cause, heretofore issued on January 18, 1994 [ante, p. 1070], is hereby discharged.
