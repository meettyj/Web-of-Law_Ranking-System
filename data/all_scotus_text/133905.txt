540 U.S. 1014     ALABAMA ET AL. v. NORTH CAROLINA.     No. 132, Orig.     Supreme Court of United States.     November 17, 2003.            11       It is ordered that Bradford R. Clark, Esq., of Washington, D. C., be appointed as Special Master in this case with authority to fix the time and conditions for the filing of additional pleadings and to direct subsequent proceedings, and with authority to summon witnesses, issue subpoenas, and take such evidence as may be introduced and such as he may deem it necessary to call for. The Special Master is directed to submit such reports as he may deem appropriate.
