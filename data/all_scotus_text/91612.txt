MR. CHIEF JUSTICE WAITE delivered the opinion of the court.
The order remanding this cause is affirmed. The petition for removal was not filed in time and the suit was not removable. Pirie v. Tvedt, 115 U.S. 41; Sloane v. Anderson, ante 275, decided at this term.
Affirmed.
