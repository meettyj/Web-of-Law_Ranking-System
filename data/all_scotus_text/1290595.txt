
(2008)
TYRONE JUDON 
v. 
UNITED STATES
No. 06-11525
Supreme Court of United States.
January 7, 2008.

The motion of petitioner for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the Eighth Circuit for further consideration in light of Kimbrough v. United States, 552 U.S. ___ (2007).
