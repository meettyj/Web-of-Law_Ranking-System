
(2008)
MICHAEL J. D'AMICO 
v. 
UNITED STATES
No. 07-7962
Supreme Court of United States.
February 19, 2008.

The motion of petitioner for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the First Circuit for further consideration in light of Gall v. United States, 552 U.S. ___ (2007).
