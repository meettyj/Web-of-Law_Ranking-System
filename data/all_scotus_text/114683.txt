    In re Disbarment of De Loach. No. D-1320.

      It is ordered that Guion T. De Loach, of Naples, Fla., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
