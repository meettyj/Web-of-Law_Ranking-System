    In re Disbarment of Micks. No. D-1354.
    Supreme Court of United States.

      11
      It is ordered that Deitra Raverne Handy Micks, of Jacksonville, Fla., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring her to show cause why she should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
