510 U.S. 1035
    In re Disbarment of Kole. No. D-1342.
    Supreme Court of United States.
    January 10, 1994.
    
      11
      It is ordered that Bernard Patrick Kole, of Havre de Grace, Md., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
