    In re Disbarment of Cooper. No. D-1349.
    Supreme Court of United States.
    January 10, 1994.
    
      11
      It is ordered that Garry Michael Cooper, of Houston, Tex., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
