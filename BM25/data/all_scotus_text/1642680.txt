
202 U.S. 59 (1906)
IOWA 
v. 
ILLINOIS.
No. 2, Original.
Supreme Court of United States.
Decree entered April 23, 1906.
IN EQUITY.
*60 PER CURIAM:
In consideration whereof and of the decision of this court reported 147 U.S. 1, it is ordered, adjudged and decreed that the boundary line between the State of Iowa and the State of Illinois is the middle of the main navigable channel of the Mississippi river at the places where the nine bridges mentioned in the pleadings cross said river.
