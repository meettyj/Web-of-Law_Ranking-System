510 U.S. 930
    In re Disbarment of Casalino. No. D-1317.
    Supreme Court of United States.
    October 18, 1993.
    
      11
      It is ordered that Leonard Louis Casalino, of Upper Marlboro, Md., be suspended from the practice of law in this Court and that a rule issue, returnable within 40 days, requiring him to show cause why he should not be disbarred from the practice of law in this Court.
